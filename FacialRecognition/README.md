## Quick walkthrough

1.) Firstly, open the FacialRecognitionWithServo folder, then the OpenCV_PhotoTaker folder, and run the photoTaker.py file.

2.) After doing so, a window will open, showing the camera feed from your personal camera.

3.) Press 's' on your keyboard to take a photo.
(Would recommend you take at least 10+ photos, all with a slightly different angle to truly train the algorithm.)

4.) The photos will be stored in (ReplaceWithName) folder in which you can press ctrl+f to search for "ReplaceWithName", in both the
photoTaker.py and main.py, to change the name to your own name.

5.) Next you will run the main.py file and this will open your camera once again and will use a training algorithm to determine.
if your face is detected from the photo, you had previously taken.
